Quiz Rules and Regulations

Welcome to the quiz! Please read and follow the rules and regulations below:

  1. Each participant must provide accurate information during registration.

  2. Participants are required to complete the quiz within the specified time limit.

  3. Any form of cheating or plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.

  4. Answers submitted after the quiz timer expires will not be considered.

  5. Participants are encouraged to answer all questions to the best of their ability.

  6. The quiz results are final and cannot be contested.

  7. Prizes, if applicable, will be awarded based on the participant's score and eligibility.
