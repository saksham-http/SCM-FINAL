How to Play

Welcome to our quiz! Here's how to play:

  1. Registration: You must be a registered participant to play the quiz. If you haven't registered yet, please do so.

  2. Logging In: Log in using your username and password on the login page.

  3. Select a Quiz: Once logged in, you'll see a list of available quizzes. Choose the quiz you want to play.

  4. Answer Questions: Read each question carefully and select your answer from the options provided. You can only select one answer per question.

  5. Submit Answers: After answering all the questions, click the "Submit" button to submit your answers for scoring.

  6. Scoring: Your score will be calculated based on the number of correct answers. The highest score wins!

  7. Enjoy the Quiz: Have fun and test your knowledge! Good luck!
